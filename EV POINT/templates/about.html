About EV Charging Station Finder

EV Charging Station Finder is a web application that helps electric vehicle (EV) drivers locate nearby charging stations. Our mission is to simplify trip planning by ensuring easy access to station availability, types of chargers, and more.

How It Works

The platform uses Google Maps API to display real-time charging station data. Users input a location, and the map visualizes nearby stations, including details like charger type, availability, and pricing (if available).
